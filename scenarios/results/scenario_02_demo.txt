Here's a detailed overview of your 5-day surf trip along the Portuguese coastline, focusing on three fantastic spots: Ericeira, Peniche/Supertubos, and Sagres/Tonel!

### Surf Spot Conditions

1. **Ericeira (Ribeira D'Ilhas)**
   - **Skill Level**: Intermediate
   - **Conditions**: Generally **challenging** with wave heights around 1.3 to 1.4 meters. Wind speeds are manageable (10-20 km/h), and the swell period is decent (8s).
   - **Safety**: Safe overall, with no unsafe hours flagged.

2. **Peniche (Supertubos)**
   - **Skill Level**: Intermediate
   - **Conditions**: Primarily **challenging** as well, with wave heights of 1.2 to 1.4 meters, and similar wind conditions.
   - **Safety**: Safe overall, with no unsafe hours flagged.

3. **Sagres (Tonel)**
   - **Skill Level**: Intermediate
   - **Conditions**: Consistently **challenging** with wave heights ranging from 1.4 meters, and good swell conditions.
   - **Safety**: Safe overall, with no unsafe hours flagged.
